2Get the clips

Manual path — in tryinfer Studio: 9:16 vertical · 5s · your chosen model. Copy each prompt, generate, download — in order, then:

oldest first = generation order; each lands on its beat with the radio set to video. Or drop files straight onto the rows (several at once fill consecutive beats).

Click any prompt to rewrite it — Save prompts keeps your wording for the Copy buttons and the AI modes above. Attach a picture or your own clip to a beat — ＋ image / ＋ video, drop a file on the row, or point at a row and paste (Ctrl+V). The radio button picks which one that beat uses — and it always ends up in the video: image = the picture IS the beat's visual (shown with gentle motion; in ⚡ AI video mode it's animated as the clip's first frame instead), video = your clip plays as-is (nothing billed for that beat). 🎥 From image writes the prompt for you: a video description of the picture.

🕘 Full history — whole saved versions of the script and prompts (each beat row has its own 🕘 for just that beat)
